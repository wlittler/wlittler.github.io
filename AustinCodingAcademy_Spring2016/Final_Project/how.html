How do I join the Electric Revolution?

Begin by reducing your dependency on fossil fuels. Invest in solar panels for your home, or purchase green energy from your local utility. Try to generate and conserve your own power as much as possible.

Adapt your lifestyle to a more sustainable, green existence. Purchase products that are energy efficient and sustainable, like LED light bulbs and bamboo-sourced products. Follow the "3 R's" - Reduce, Reuse, Recycle. Study green technology any way you can. Advocate for it's propagation, and share with everyone you can.

Buy an electric vehicle, or convert your favorite vehicle to an EV. As more adopt electric vehicles, the choice expands and the price falls. Abandon fossil fuels.

Adopt an electric lifestyle. Don't use disposable batteries, use high-quality rechargables (like Lithium-based batteries). Use electric lawn equipment. Make all your transport electric or green - ride a bicycle, buy an electric scooter. The transition is easier than you think - cell phones replaced home phones in less than 20 years, entirely because wide adoption reduced cost to a universally affordable level.
