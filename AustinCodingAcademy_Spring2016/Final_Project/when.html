When should I join the Electric Revolution?

Right now! In fact, you already have - the technology powering the cell phone in your pocket is a key driver to this trend. The sooner more adopt green technology, rechargable batteries, and perhaps most importantly right now, electric vehicles, the cheaper the technology becomes. As it becomes cheaper, more adopt the technology, and it becomes cheaper still, until it has disseminated globally.

The timing couldn't be any better. There has been an explosion of green technology in the last decade, and once unknown companies focusing on green technology are the rockstars of our day.

Solar installations are popping up all over the country, don't get left behind!

The time to act is now! Start small, work your way to bigger goals. Buy an electric scooter today, get a Tesla later.
