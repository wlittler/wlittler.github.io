Who do I support?

One of the most important questions is who to support in the Electric Revolution. There are many considerations to make, and many must be tailored to your specific situation.

Choose companies that have a strong vision for the future. Avoid companies that are in green technology just to "ride the wave" and maximize their profits. While any publicly traded company must by definition consider its stakeholders, the primary consideration should be the end goal.

Choose products that fit your lifestyle. Not everyone can afford a brand new Tesla, but there are plenty of alternatives. A lithium-powered electric bicycle can be purchased for as little as $1000. Just participating is advancing the movement.

Who to support is a personal choice, and must be based upon what makes most sense for you, but do whatever you can and Join the Electric Revolution!
