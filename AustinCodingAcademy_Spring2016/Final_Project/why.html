Why should I join the Electric Revolution?

Our world was once beautiful, pristine, uncorrupted by human influence. Crystal clear springs erupted from mountainsides, animals roamed the countryside in peace, birds soared through beautiful blue skies.

As time progressed, human beings began to leverage their scientific knowledge to unlock stored energy, in the form of fossil fuels. The unfortunate side effect of this was pollution, on a massive scale. Clouds of noxious gasses began to appear over our cities, and our health suffered.

If humanity continues upon this path, we will poison our oceans beyond repair, choke the sunlight from our skies, and transform our world into a barren wasteland, incapable of supporting life.

The future does not have to be bleak, it can instead be beautiful. The key is in your hands. Humanity has the capability to transform itself into a model of renewable energy efficiency, benefitting both the environment and all of society.
