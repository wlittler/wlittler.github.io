What is body scrub? (click to expand)

I have come to realize that there are a lot of folks out there who don’t know what a body scrub is. Man oh man are you missing out! Whether you prefer yours made with sugar or salt, body scrubs are full of benefits. All AmaZen Scrubs are made using USDA Organic cane sugar which works to exfoliate dead skin cells. My special blend of carrier oils hydrate your skin. Many people have the misconception that scrubs leave your skin feeling oily and icky. This is NOT the case with my scrubs. You will leave your shower hydrated, silky smooth, and ready to take on the day. Use before shaving to ensure a clean, close shave but don’t forget to rinse your razor well after use to prevent clogging… cuz a clogged razor is just! All of my AmaZen body scrubs contain essential oils to help boost moods and take you to your happy place. Say goodbye to dry and flaky skin with any of my AmaZen scents.
