About Leia Morris PT, DPT

Leia Morris, PT, DPT is an Austin native who studied at the University of Texas and graduated summa cum laude and with Special Honors in the Plan II (Liberal Arts) Honors degree program. She was accepted to Duke University School of Medicine and completed her Doctor of Physical Therapy degree in 2003.

Since receiving her license and degree, Dr. Morris has completed scores of hours of additional training in the fields of women’s health and chronic pain. Some of her post-graduate training courses have included myofascial release, pelvic floor evaluation and treatment, and differential diagnosis of chronic pelvic pain. She reports she is drawn to these areas because she enjoys complexity and challenge.

She finds physical therapy most rewarding she can work with a patient who had thought they would never be able to feel better and be able to restore them to enjoying movement, activity, and hope.
